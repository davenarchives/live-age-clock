About Live Age Clock

The Live Age Clock is a web application designed to help users calculate and display their age in years, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. It provides a simple and interactive way for users to understand the passage of time since their birth.

How to Use

  1. Select your birth month from the dropdown menu.
  2. Choose your birth day from the available options.
  3. Enter your birth year in the text input field.
  4. (Optional) Provide your time of birth using the time input field.
  5. Click the "Start" button to begin the live countdown of your age.
  6. If you wish to reset, click the "Reset" button to return to the input form.

This tool is great for those who want to see just how much time has passed since their birth in a fun and engaging way.
